### Research Interests

I develop statistical methods for analyzing complex, high-dimensional data using modern machine learning models. I am particularly interested in *distribution-free* and *model-agnostic* approaches that can produce precise statistical inferences under defensible assumptions.
I sometimes also work as a consultant and expert collaborator in both academic and industry settings, helping to design reproducible data analyses, quantify uncertainty in complex models, and communicate results clearly to diverse audiences.
