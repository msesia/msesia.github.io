Two of our papers received top honors this year at the 14th [Symposium on Conformal and Probabilistic Prediction with Applications (COPA)](https://copa-conference.com/).

**Best Paper**: Conformal Survival Bands for Risk Screening under Right-Censoring, with Vladimir Svetnik.

**Best Poster** (one of two awards): Conformal Classification with New Labels, with my PhD student Tianmin Xie, Ziyi Liang, and Stefano Favaro.
